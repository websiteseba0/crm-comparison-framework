# Small Business CRM

Small business CRM software helps startups and growing teams organize contacts, manage sales workflows, and automate customer communication.

This guide explains how to choose the best CRM for small businesses.

---

## What Small Businesses Need in a CRM

The best CRM systems for small businesses prioritize:

- ease of use
- fast onboarding
- simple automation
- affordable pricing
- scalability
- integrations
- sales pipeline visibility

---

## CRM Features That Matter Most

### Contact Management

A CRM should make it easy to:

- organize leads
- track conversations
- manage customer history
- segment contacts

### Sales Pipeline Management

Small teams benefit from:

- visual pipelines
- deal tracking
- follow-up reminders
- task automation

### CRM Automation

Automation features can reduce repetitive work by handling:

- email sequences
- task reminders
- lead assignments
- reporting updates

---

## Popular Small Business CRM Platforms

Common CRM choices include:

- HubSpot CRM
- Pipedrive
- Zoho CRM
- Freshsales
- Monday CRM
- Close CRM

---

## CRM Pricing Considerations

When comparing CRM pricing, small businesses should evaluate:

- per-user pricing
- onboarding costs
- upgrade paths
- automation limits
- contact limits
