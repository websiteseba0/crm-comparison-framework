## CRM Workflow Questions

### Sales Workflow

- Does the CRM support custom pipelines?
- Can workflows be automated?
- Is forecasting included?

### Integrations

- Does the CRM integrate with email tools?
- Are API integrations available?
- Does it connect with marketing platforms?

### Reporting

- Are dashboards customizable?
- Can reports be automated?
- Are KPIs easy to track?
