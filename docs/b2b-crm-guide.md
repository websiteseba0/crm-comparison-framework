# B2B CRM Guide

---

## Common B2B CRM Use Cases

### Sales Pipeline Management

B2B sales teams use CRM platforms to:

- track deals
- manage sales stages
- forecast revenue
- monitor conversion rates

### Lead Management

CRM systems help organize:

- inbound leads
- outbound campaigns
- lead qualification
- follow-up workflows

### CRM Automation

Modern CRM software supports:

- automated follow-ups
- lead routing
- workflow triggers
- reporting automation

## How to Compare B2B CRM Software
