# B2C CRM Guide
---

### Omnichannel Communication

The best B2C CRM platforms centralize communication across:

- email
- chat
- SMS
- social media
- customer support channels

---

## Best B2C CRM Platforms

Popular B2C CRM software includes:

- HubSpot CRM
- Salesforce Marketing Cloud
- Zoho CRM
- Freshsales
- ActiveCampaign
- Klaviyo

---

## How to Compare B2C CRM Software

When comparing B2C CRM platforms, evaluate:

- automation capabilities
- customer segmentation
- campaign workflows
- personalization features
- integrations
- reporting dashboards
- pricing scalability
- ease of use

---

## B2C CRM for Small Businesses

Smaller businesses should prioritize:

- affordable pricing
- simple onboarding
- automation templates
- fast implementation
- intuitive dashboards
